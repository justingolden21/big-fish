How to Play / Help

How to Play

You start the game with 1 small fish .

Your fish earn you coins when they're full.

Your fish won't starve when they're hungry, they just won't give you coins.

Your fish eat food which costs coins .

You can purchase food and fish under the Purchase tab.

You can purchase medium fish which eat small fish and earn you lots of coins .

You can purchase large fish which eat medium fish and earn you lots more coins .

You only have room in your aquarium for so many fish ! You can purchase new aquariums to hold more fish .

You can sell stuff you don't need for a lower return value.

You can purchase buildings to produce stuff for you .

You can purchase banks to automatically buy and sell items for you .

Snowflakes multiply the number of coins your fish earn you. Penguins make 1 snowflake per min and cost an increasing amount of snowflakes.

Penguin Hatcheries will hatch a new penguin every minute and cost an increasing amount of snowflakes.

Snow Banks will can automatically buy and sell penguins and penguin hatcheries, as well as converting coins to snowflakes. They cost an increasing amount of snowflakes.

Build your aquarium up and make as many coins as you can!

Keep playing to unlock more features
