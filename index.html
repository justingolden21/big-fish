How to Play

You start the game with 1 small fish .

The fish earns you money when it's full.

The fish eats food which costs money .

You can purchase food and fish under the Purchase tab.

You can purchase medium fish which eat small fish and earn you lots of money .

You can purchase large fish which eat medium fish and earn you lots more money .

You only have room in your aquarium for so many fish ! You can purchase new aquariums to hold more fish .

You can sell stuff you don't need for a lower return value.

You can purchase buildings to produce stuff for you .

Build your aquarium up and make as much money as you can!

Keep playing to unlock more features
