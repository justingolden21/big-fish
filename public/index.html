How to Play / Help

How to Play

You start the game with 1 small fish .

Your fish earn you shells when they're full.

Your fish won't starve when they're hungry, they just won't give you shells.

Your fish eat food which costs shells .

You can purchase food and fish under the Purchase tab.

You can purchase medium fish which eat small fish and earn you lots of shells .

You can purchase large fish which eat medium fish and earn you lots more shells .

You only have room in your aquarium for so many fish ! You can purchase new aquariums to hold more fish .

You can sell stuff you don't need for a lower return value.

You can purchase buildings to produce stuff for you .

You can purchase banks to automatically buy and sell items for you .

Stars multiply the number of shells your fish earn you. Pufferfishes make 1 star per min and cost an increasing amount of stars.

Pufferfish Hatcheries will hatch a new pufferfish every minute and cost an increasing amount of stars.

Star Banks will can automatically buy and sell pufferfishes and pufferfish hatcheries, as well as converting shells to stars. They cost an increasing amount of stars.

Build your aquarium up and make as many shells as you can!

Keep playing to unlock more features
